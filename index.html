About

I research how adult platforms handle user data — trackers, cookies, browser fingerprinting, keystroke capture, and session recording. My goal is to give people the information they need to make safer choices online.

My research covers 1,000+ adult sites scanned with The Markup's Blacklight tool, analyzing tracking behavior most users never see.

Current Project

167+ in-depth reviews live. 1,007 sites in the privacy database.

Key Findings
